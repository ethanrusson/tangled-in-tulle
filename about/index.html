About

Tangled in Tulle was born in 2012 as a senior project in Emily’s basement bedroom. What started as a simple requirement to graduate turned into a thriving small business, met with lots of interest and success. When Emily headed off to college that fall, her mom, Esther, took over, continuing the dream from a dedicated room in their home. Over the next five years, business flourished, and in 2018, we opened our storefront.

Over the last three years, we’ve expanded into bridal gowns, helping hundreds of brides find their dream dresses for their special day. Whether it was for a wedding, a prom, or another milestone, we’ve loved being part of so many amazing celebrations, helping our clients feel as beautiful on the outside as they are on the inside.

Now, we’re excited to announce an important change in our business. Tangled in Tulle Bridal Outlet has shifted its focus exclusively to bridal gowns. We’ve made the decision to no longer carry prom dresses, allowing us to dedicate ourselves fully to helping brides find affordable, high-quality wedding dresses at outlet prices. Our gowns are available at three price points—$300, $500, and $750—ensuring every bride can find her perfect dress within her budget.

We’re thrilled for this new chapter and grateful for all the incredible clients we’ve had the pleasure of serving. A dress for every bride—and we can't wait to help you find yours!
