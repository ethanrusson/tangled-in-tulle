About

Tangled in Tulle started in 2012 in Emily’s basement bedroom as her senior project requirement to graduate. The small business was met with lots of interest and success, so when Emily headed off to college the following fall, her mom, Esther, continued from a dedicated room in their home. For 5 more years, business grew from that bedroom until we opened our current storefront in 2018.

In the last 3 years, we’ve expanded into bridal gowns and helped hundreds of clients find their dream dresses for any event. For whatever special day our clients are preparing for, we love helping them feel look as beautiful on the outside as they are on the inside!
